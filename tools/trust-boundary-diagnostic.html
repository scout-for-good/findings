Trust Boundary Diagnostic

Where are the hidden assumptions in your agent architecture?

Every AI agent architecture makes trust assumptions — about identity, memory, tool access, delegation, and values. Most of these assumptions are invisible. They were made implicitly during design and frozen into the architecture, where they function without being examined.

This diagnostic asks 12 questions about your agent's architecture. For each answer, it identifies the trust boundary — the point where an assumption could break — and maps the frozen value choice embedded in your design.

Based on data from the 2026 State of AI Agent Security Report: 81% of teams have deployed agents, but only 14.4% have full security approval. 88% report security incidents. The gap between governance principles and architectural reality is where failures happen.
